# Brand Language

## Brand Position

ChildNotes is a companion for families to capture, organize and understand childhood memories.

The brand is built around one simple idea:

> Every small moment deserves to be remembered.

## Brand Personality

### Warm

The product should feel welcoming and safe. It should create emotional comfort instead of pressure.

### Gentle

Interactions should respect parents' limited time and attention.

### Trustworthy

Family memories are private and meaningful. The experience must communicate reliability.

### Intelligent

AI should quietly help users discover meaning from memories, not replace human emotion.

## Visual Character

ChildNotes should avoid:

- Overly cartoonish children's interfaces
- Excessive decoration
- Cold enterprise software feeling

The preferred direction is:

- Modern family product
- Calm technology
- Emotional simplicity
